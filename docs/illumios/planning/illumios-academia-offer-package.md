## Offer Snapshot

- Offer name: `Illumios Academia`
- Public subtitle: `AI for Small Business Owners: Your First 30 Days`
- Format: 4 live sessions over 2 weeks
- Delivery: small-group live cohort
- Default price: `$497`
- Primary CTA: `Apply now`
- Enrollment path: website application -> fit quiz -> enrollment call -> payment -> onboarding

## Final Promise

**In 4 live sessions, small business owners will identify their best AI opportunities, build one working workflow, and leave with a practical 30-day plan without needing to become technical experts.**

Short public version:

**Learn how to use AI in your business in 30 days by building one practical workflow you can actually keep using.**

## Best-Fit Participant

This program is best for:

- small business owners, operators, and lean-team founders
- service-based or admin-heavy businesses
- people who know AI could help but have not turned it into a repeatable habit yet
- people who want guided implementation, not endless tutorials

This program is not for:

- highly technical builders who want advanced automation training
- businesses looking for a done-for-you agency
- people who only want free AI tips without implementation work

## Participant Outcomes

By the end of the program, each participant should leave with:

- 3 prioritized AI use cases that fit their real business
- 1 simple tool and prompt stack they can use immediately
- 1 documented AI-assisted workflow or repeatable operating process
- 1 practical 30-day implementation plan with weekly actions
- more confidence using AI in daily business work without feeling overwhelmed

## 4-Session Structure

Each session is designed as:

- 20 to 30 minutes of teaching and examples
- 30 to 40 minutes of guided working time
- 15 to 20 minutes of live feedback and Q&A
- one concrete deliverable completed before the next session

### Session 1: Find Your First AI Wins

Purpose:
Help participants see where AI fits in their real business and choose the highest-value starting points.

Focus:

- where small businesses lose time and energy
- what AI is good at and where it is not
- how to spot repetitive, admin-heavy, high-friction tasks
- how to choose use cases that are realistic, useful, and worth testing now

Participant outcome:

- 3 prioritized AI use cases for their business

### Session 2: Use the Right Tools and Prompts

Purpose:
Help participants stop guessing and start using a simple tool stack with better prompts.

Focus:

- choosing tools without getting lost in software overload
- prompt structure for business tasks
- improving weak outputs into useful outputs
- examples for communication, operations, and marketing work

Participant outcome:

- 1 simple tool stack and 3 reusable prompts tailored to their work

### Session 3: Build Your First AI Workflow

Purpose:
Turn one repeated task into a practical AI-assisted process participants can keep using.

Focus:

- turning a messy task into a repeatable sequence
- deciding where AI fits and where human review stays
- documenting the workflow clearly enough to repeat
- keeping the first implementation simple instead of overbuilding

Participant outcome:

- 1 working AI-assisted workflow or repeatable process

### Session 4: Make It Stick for 30 Days

Purpose:
Turn early momentum into an adoption plan that survives after the live sessions end.

Focus:

- weekly usage rhythm
- measuring whether the workflow is actually saving time
- deciding what to continue, improve, or drop
- planning the next 30 days without adding unnecessary complexity

Participant outcome:

- 1 personal 30-day implementation plan

## Enrollment Flow

### Public Flow

1. Prospect sees the invitation and understands the promise quickly.
2. Prospect completes the website application.
3. Prospect completes the fit quiz so Illumios can understand role, pain points, and readiness.
4. Qualified prospect books an enrollment call.
5. On the call, Illumios confirms fit, explains outcomes, answers questions, and invites the right participant to join at founding-member pricing.
6. Participant pays and is tagged as enrolled.
7. Participant receives onboarding details, session dates, expectations, and a simple pre-work prompt.

### Enrollment Call Goal

The enrollment call is not sold as a separate product. Its purpose is to:

- confirm the participant is a fit
- anchor the offer around outcomes instead of abstract AI education
- reduce hesitation by making the path concrete
- close enrollment cleanly

### Simple Onboarding Expectation

Before Session 1, participants should arrive with:

- one list of recurring tasks or busywork in their business
- one business process they would love to simplify
- a willingness to implement one practical change during the program
