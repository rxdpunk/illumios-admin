# Illumios Operating Directives

Last updated: 2026-04-14  
Status: Active source of truth for repo memory and cross-thread alignment

## Current Priority

Illumios' current company priority is to launch and fill `Illumios Academia`.

This is the main offer that should be reflected across:

- website
- admin
- quiz

Discovery calls, strategy sessions, and custom help support this offer. They are not the primary front-door message right now.

## Canonical Public Positioning

Illumios is an education-first AI company for small business owners.

Canonical positioning:

**Illumios helps small business owners use AI to save time, reduce busywork, and build smarter workflows without needing to become technical experts.**

Avoid positioning Illumios as:

- a generic done-for-you AI agency
- a broad "we do everything AI" consultancy
- a tool-first company without a clear educational outcome

## Canonical Offer

- Offer name: `Illumios Academia`
- Public subtitle: `AI for Small Business Owners: Your First 30 Days`
- Canonical offer promise: `In 4 live sessions, small business owners will identify their best AI opportunities, build one working workflow, and leave with a practical 30-day plan without needing to become technical experts.`
- Format: 4 live sessions over 2 weeks
- Pricing stance: founding-member pricing
- Default target price: `$497`
- Test range: `$297` to `$697`
- Default enrollment path: website application -> fit quiz -> enrollment call -> payment -> onboarding

Primary participant outcome:

- 3 relevant AI use cases
- 1 working workflow or repeatable process
- 1 personal 30-day action plan

## Cross-Surface Directives

### Website

- Lead with `Illumios Academia` as the current offer.
- Keep the promise concrete, outcome-based, and small-business specific.
- Use discovery calls as the enrollment conversation for the program, not as the primary product.

### Admin

- Prioritize lead tracking, cohort operations, onboarding, attendance, feedback, testimonials, and follow-up tied to the program.
- Any dashboards or workflows should make it easy to see who is quiz-qualified, booked, enrolled, attended, and ready for upsell.

### Quiz

- The quiz exists to diagnose fit, surface useful AI starting points, and move qualified people toward `Illumios Academia`.
- Quiz outcomes should feed tags, segmentation, and booking or application flows aligned to the main offer.
- Quiz copy should reinforce the education-first positioning for small business owners.
