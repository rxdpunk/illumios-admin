# Illumios LLC Formation Decision Memo

Date: April 14, 2026
Audience: Co-founders
Purpose: Short-form decision memo for founder review

Status: Reference memo. Current execution memory lives in [illumios-operating-directives.md](/Users/sposato/Dev_Projects/illumios/planning/illumios-operating-directives.md).

## Decision

Form `Illumios LLC` in **New Jersey**.

Do **not** form in Wyoming as the default path.

## Why This Is the Best Current Choice

The company is currently being operated primarily from New Jersey.

- Steve lives in New Jersey.
- Steve is full-time on Illumios.
- Steve is currently running day-to-day operations.
- Steve is the likely public-facing founder and primary operator.

Because of that, New Jersey is the most defensible home state for:

- company formation
- tax and employer registration
- operational control
- future scaling

## Why Not Wyoming

Wyoming is not inherently wrong, but on the current facts it likely adds complexity rather than reducing it.

If Illumios forms in Wyoming but is actually operated from New Jersey, New Jersey would likely still remain a compliance state. That means Wyoming could become an extra filing layer instead of a simplification.

Practical downside:

- more paperwork
- another registered agent
- another annual compliance calendar
- more opportunity for preventable administrative mistakes

## Why Not South Carolina

South Carolina is relevant but does not appear to be the best initial formation state.

- Sunshine lives in South Carolina.
- Sunshine is currently full-time at TurnPoint Solutions.
- Sunshine is not currently the primary full-time operating founder for Illumios.

On the present facts, South Carolina looks more like a state to monitor than the best place to form the company.

## Important Founder-Risk Issue

The biggest non-state issue is Sunshine's current employment at TurnPoint Solutions.

That creates potential issues around:

- moonlighting restrictions
- confidentiality
- invention assignment
- public visibility
- title and authority design

This does not change the recommendation that New Jersey is the best formation state. It does mean governance and documentation should be handled carefully.

## Recommended Near-Term Structure

- Form the company in New Jersey.
- Use an operating agreement that reflects actual founder roles.
- Steve should be documented as the primary day-to-day operator and public-facing founder.
- Sunshine's role should be structured thoughtfully while she remains employed elsewhere.

## Questions To Confirm With Counsel

1. Whether Sunshine's current level of participation creates any immediate South Carolina registration issue.
2. Whether the company should be manager-managed.
3. What founder title, equity, and vesting structure best fits the current operating reality.
4. Whether Sunshine's current employment documents create any limitations on role visibility or authority.

## Final Recommendation

The cleanest current path is:

**Form Illumios as a New Jersey LLC now, then reassess South Carolina only if Sunshine's operating role expands.**
