# 90-Day Launch Plan

**Illumios — Education-First AI Training Company**
Revised: April 14, 2026 (originally revised April 9, now aligned to current directives)

## Overview

This 90-day plan takes Illumios from a working foundation to a repeatable, referral-driven education business. Each phase builds on the last — finish the foundation, get in front of people, convert the first paying students, then build the flywheel.

Illumios is an education-first AI training company for small business owners. The current live priority is `Illumios Academia`, with strategy sessions and discovery calls supporting that offer rather than leading it.

## Phase 2 — In-Person Funnel Ignition (Days 15–30)

The business runs on in-person trust-building. Phase 2 is about getting in front of actual owners.

### Local Presence
- **BNI:** attend 2 chapter meetings as a guest; deliver a 60-second intro built around the education pitch (not a services pitch)
- **Chamber of Commerce:** join local chapter; attend first networking event
- **Library partnerships:** book at least one free "AI for Small Business Owners" talk

### Lead Magnet Live
- Finalize the **Pain Point Audit** — 5 questions, delivered either as the quiz funnel or a live workshop tool
- Capture email + business info into GHL; tag and segment by audit result
- Keep the quiz aligned to `Illumios Academia` and route qualified leads toward enrollment

### Warm Network Activation
- Personal outreach to the 232-contact warm list — educational, not salesy. Ask who'd want to attend a free intro session.
- 5 coffee chats with local business owners to pressure-test language, offer shape, and pricing sensitivity

### Digital Support
- LinkedIn: 15–20 ideal-ICP connection requests per week with personal notes
- 1–2 short educational posts per week (what AI actually does for an SMB, myths, practical wins)

**Phase 2 targets:**
- 2 in-person talks booked or delivered
- Pain Point Audit live and collecting responses
- 3–5 discovery calls booked from combined channels

## Phase 3 — First Paying Students (Days 31–60)

Convert attention into paid engagements. Learn what people actually want to buy.

### Convert
- Run follow-up calls with every Pain Point Audit taker and every talk attendee
- Close first paid **strategy sessions** (1:1 consults) — use these to learn price sensitivity and refine the Academia outline
- Enroll the first 3–5 students into **Illumios Academia**
  - Default price target is `$497`, with deliberate testing around that anchor if needed
- Begin placing the **GHL affiliate** at the relevant point in the curriculum

### Deliver
- Run the first cohort live or async; record sessions; capture testimonials and case-study language
- Weekly office hours or Q&A calls with paying students
- Document the delivery SOP as you go — this becomes the Phase 4 scalability asset

### Continue Phase 2 motions
- Keep booking talks and coffee chats
- Ask every happy student/attendee for one referral and one review

**Phase 3 targets:**
- First paid revenue (strategy sessions + Academia enrollments)
- First 3–5 `Illumios Academia` students enrolled
- First published case study or testimonial
- Pricing experiments documented (what converted, what didn't)

---

## Phase 4 — Flywheel & Academia Scale (Days 61–90)

Turn Phase 3's learnings into a repeatable system.

### Lock In the Funnel
- Repeat the Talk → Pain Point Audit → Discovery Call → Enrollment loop with at least 2 new venues
- Codify the "Audit Breakfast" format as the flagship top-of-funnel play
- Finalize pricing based on Phase 3 data — replace "pricing in flux" with a committed menu

### Academia v1 → v1.1
- Ship the first full Academia module set (Mindset → first automation win)
- Integrate GHL affiliate link at Module 4
- Open enrollment publicly (email list, LinkedIn, BNI network)

### Content & Authority
- Publish 1 case study from a Phase 3 student
- Record a short recap video or podcast episode per talk delivered
- Begin building an evergreen library on illumios.com

### Systems
- Move ongoing student support into a repeatable structure (cohort calls, Slack/Discord, or async workflow)
- Add one team capacity lever: Sunshine-led content, a VA for admin, or outsourced editing
- Verify pipeline naming stays aligned with Illumios branding

**Phase 4 targets:**
- Funnel runs without founder-energy spikes: at least one full cycle happens from a talk neither founder personally hustled for
- Committed public pricing menu
- Academia open for ongoing enrollment
- First affiliate commission earned

---

## What's Intentionally Not in This Plan

- **Specific monthly revenue targets.** Revenue targets will be set after Phase 3 pricing experiments produce real conversion data.
- **Fixed course pricing.** The team is actively navigating the offer menu. This plan treats pricing as a variable to be solved in Phase 3, not a constraint set in Phase 1.
